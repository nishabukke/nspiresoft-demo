Promotional or Transactional, Which SMS Route Should I Choose?

Choosing the right SMS route depends on your communication goals. Promotional SMS is ideal for marketing messages, offers, and bulk campaigns aimed at engaging customers and driving sales. In contrast, Transactional SMS is used for sending critical alerts, OTPs, order confirmations, and service updates that require immediate delivery and high trust. Understanding the difference ensures your messages reach the right audience effectively and comply with regulations.

Promotional SMS

Promotional SMS are marketing messages sent to a wide audience to promote products, services, discounts, or events. These messages are designed to attract new customers, boost sales, and increase brand awareness, usually sent during specific campaign periods.

Transactional SMS

Transactional SMS are automated, triggered messages that provide important information related to transactions or services. Examples include OTPs (One-Time Passwords), order confirmations, delivery updates, and alerts. These messages are time-sensitive and usually mandatory for customer communication.

Business SMS

Business SMS encompasses both promotional and transactional messages tailored specifically for corporate communications. It includes customer engagement, internal notifications, service updates, and marketing campaigns—helping businesses maintain effective and reliable communication with their audience.

FAQ

Frequently asked Questions

What is SMS marketing?

SMS marketing involves sending promotional or transactional messages to customers via text to engage and promote your brand.

How do I build an SMS subscriber list?

Collect numbers through opt-in forms, website sign-ups, in-store promotions, or events to build a compliant subscriber list.

What is the difference between promotional and transactional SMS?

Promotional SMS are marketing messages sent in bulk, while transactional SMS are time-sensitive alerts like OTPs or order updates.

How can I measure the success of my SMS campaigns?

Track metrics like delivery rates, click-through rates, conversions, and ROI to evaluate campaign performance.
