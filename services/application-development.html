FAQ

Frequently asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

Development time typically ranges from 2 to 6 months, depending on the app’s features, number of screens, testing needs, and third-party integrations.

Should I build for Android or iOS first?

It depends on your target audience and budget. iOS is often preferred for high-earning users and faster development, while Android reaches a broader global market.

What technologies are used to build apps?

Apps can be built using native languages (Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android) or cross-platform frameworks like Flutter, React Native, and Xamarin.

Will I be able to update the app after it's launched?

Yes. Apps can and should be updated regularly to fix bugs, improve performance, and introduce new features. App stores allow easy update deployment.
