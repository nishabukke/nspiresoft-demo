FAQ

Frequently asked Questions

What types of emails should I send?

Common types include newsletters, promotional offers, welcome emails, product updates, and event invitations.

How can I improve my email open rates?

Use compelling subject lines, personalize emails, send at optimal times, and ensure your content is relevant and valuable to your audience.

How often should I send marketing emails?

Frequency depends on your audience and goals but typically ranges from weekly to monthly to maintain engagement without overwhelming recipients.

What is email marketing and why is it important?

Email marketing is a direct digital communication strategy used to promote products, services, or build relationships. It’s important because it delivers personalized content directly to your audience’s inbox, driving engagement and conversions.
